Hi! My name is Arunima.

A little bit about myself: I'm currently a grad student at the University of Michigan studying Human-Computer Interaction. I've always loved reading and losing myself in other people's worlds, stories, and lives. In fact, as a middle schooler, I used to always have a book in the back of my mom's car, and on the 25 minute drives to and from school I would be engrossed in my current read - completely ignoring my mom! Unfortunately, over the course of the first three years of undergrad, I found myself not making time to read, and I wasn't able to enjoy one of my favorite hobbies. But when the pandemic hit and I had more time on my hands than I ever could have imagined, I quickly reignited my love and passion for reading, have been discovering and re-discovering some of favorite books.

To share my passion, and ignite a little light of passion in all of you, I give you my book nook! I hope you enjoy looking through my favorite books, my last reads, and my reading tips & tricks. Thank you for joining me on my reading journey, and I am honored to be a part of yours.
